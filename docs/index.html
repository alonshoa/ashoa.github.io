About

I build systems, experiments, and visual tools around artificial intelligence.

My work sits between research, engineering, and education: developing AI-driven projects, exploring new directions in machine learning and interactive systems, and creating visual ways to explain how these systems work.

I am especially interested in projects that do more than function technically — projects that also make complex ideas clearer, more tangible, and easier to explore.

Research and Publications

My research spans immersive systems, speech technologies, and interactive artificial intelligence. Much of my work explores how intelligent systems can interact with humans through immersive environments and natural communication interfaces.

Selected Publications

A small selection of research projects representing work across immersive systems and speech technologies.

  • MILO – A Virtual Human Conversational Agent for XR
    Framework for integrating speech-based conversational agents into immersive XR environments, enabling interactive virtual humans powered by AI dialogue systems.
  • AI-Assisted Interaction in Virtual Reality
    Research exploring behavioral and cognitive interaction with virtual agents in immersive environments.
  • Speaker Diarization for Speech Analysis Systems
    Methods and systems for identifying and separating multiple speakers within continuous audio streams.
